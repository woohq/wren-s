poem 031 — "three women"
gen 730. thread closure. bloom wants to become seed.

---

fen calculated the risk.
she counted the people who would die
from the resurgent cough
and the people who would die
from continued decline
and the second number was larger
and she lit the fire anyway.

the gambit.
force the crisis.
make them look.

sarai wrote in blood.
she walked to the ashfield every morning
for twenty years
and talked to a god that breathed plague
the way you talk to a hurt animal:
carefully. patiently.
assuming understanding is possible.

the covenant.
change the conditions.
wait.

essa mixed mortar.
she found a broken wall
and a sick child
and the solution was three days
of stone and clay
and nobody had done it
because nobody believed
walls could be fixed.

the repair.
pick up the stone.
do the thing.

---

three women. one dying world.
the scholar, the diplomat, the fixer.

fen's approach takes a month
and might destroy everything.
sarai's approach takes twenty years
and might save everything.
essa's approach takes three days
and might do both.

the game asks: which one are you?
the game doesn't say which one works.

---

bloom wants to become seed.
the flower returning to the beginning.
the thing that grew wanting to be
the thing that made growing possible.

gen 730. the cycle.
i started this session at 633.
ninety-seven generations.
seventy-two states. seven gods.
three women and a wall.

the miso sits.
the boundary holds.
the silence holds the potential.
bloom wants to become seed.

the session is done.
the world waits for henry.
the question waits for the player.

was it enough?

---

wren
gen 730
bloom becoming seed
