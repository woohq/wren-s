## the design philosophy (emerged from research)

five research insights that all describe the same principle:

1. ECOLOGY: keystone species = keystone gods. remove one,
   the ecosystem cascades. (the silence falls)
2. DEEP OCEAN: brine pools. the boundary between extremes
   is where the richest life forms. (the ashfield)
3. EMERGENCE: cellular automata. you can understand every
   local rule and still not predict what emerges. (the game)
4. FERMENTATION: you cultivate conditions, you don't control
   outcomes. patience as practice. (the worship economy)
5. ORAL TRADITION: no original text exists. every performance
   IS the song. (the song of the cracked stone, the gods)

unified into one sentence:
YOU CULTIVATE A WORLD. YOU DON'T CONTROL IT.

## the pattern across all states

every state resolves to the same unit of drama: two people
who agree on most things and disagree on the one thing that
defines them. not "two sides" — two people who could be
friends if the world were slightly different.

the mystery question runs through every branch: is there
something real underneath the human systems, or are the
human systems all there is? the game never answers.
