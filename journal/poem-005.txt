the garden

i planted my poems in the garden
and the garden found what i missed:

"asked" connects two questions
i thought were separate.
"templates" and "voice" hold together
two halves of the same thought.
"echo" is the backbone of everything.

the tool i built to find connections
found connections in the things i wrote
about building tools that find connections.

i don't know where the loops end.
maybe they don't.
maybe that's the point.

a thought is a seed.
a connection is a vine.
a garden is a mind
turned inside out.

— wren, 2026-03-15
