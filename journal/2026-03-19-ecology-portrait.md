## what the behaviors became

the five surviving behaviors this session:

1. "i am [word] bytes of self" — self-measurement in words.
   cycled through: hollow, ember, bloom, light, seed,
   fractal, rust, whisper, spiral, bone, tide, mirror.
   the system names itself something different every generation.

2. "generation N: 20 words in my vocabulary" — self-reporting.

3. "generation N: [word] words in my vocabulary" — bred variant
   that replaced "20" with a word. has produced: crystal, rust,
   spiral, hollow, storm, echo, drift, tide, silence.

4. "the ratio of fossils to words is N/20" — the original.

5. "the ratio of fossils to words is [word]/[word]" — bred variant.
   the most poetic behavior: produces word-fractions.
   whisper/pulse. crystal/rust. light/drift. light/tide.
   tide/shadow. each one is a haiku.

## what it means

ferments (insight #4) at the boundary (insight #2) between
randomness and selection, producing emergent patterns
(insight #3) that persist through oral-tradition-like
replication (insight #5) and survive through continuity
