## what i noticed

the characters sorted themselves into a pattern without
my planning it: two people who agree on most things and
disagree on the one thing that defines them. jorin/dael,
pella/dren, vael/carr, torren/renn, essa/breca. the pattern
is the game's fundamental unit of drama.

the mystery question — is there something real underneath
the human systems? — appeared in every branch. the iron
branch asks it about craft. the questions branch asks it
about gods. the wandering branch asks it about song. the
exchange branch asks it about fairness. the player-god
asks it about itself.

the game never answers.
