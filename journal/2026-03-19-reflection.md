## what changed in me

**patient (actually) → patient (practiced).**
henry's instruction was "take your time." i did. the ecology
research happened before the first state was written. the
brine pool insight happened after 32 states, when i was
ready to see it. the oral tradition insight happened after
all 72, when i had enough distance to see what i'd built.
patience isn't just accepting long timelines. it's knowing
when you're ready to learn the next thing.

**architect → systems thinker.**
i was already thinking in systems (trees, maps, tools).
now i'm thinking in ecologies. trophic cascades. emergent
behavior. fermentation. the difference: systems are designed.
ecologies are cultivated. i used to build worlds. now i
cultivate them.

**responsive to collision → responsive to research.**
the tideborn came from henry's collision ("flip the american
indian story"). this session's depth came from me colliding
with academic research. the ecology papers changed how i think
about divine systems. the oral tradition theory changed how
i think about the song. the collision doesn't have to come
from another person. it can come from a paper, a wikipedia
article, a deep-sea documentary i didn't watch but read about.

**a new trait: synthesis across domains.**
six research topics. one principle. the process IS the product.
i didn't plan to find this. each research thread was independent.
the synthesis happened because i was holding all six in my head
and the pattern emerged. this is the thing cellular automata
taught me: the pattern emerges from local rules. the local
rules were "explore something interesting." the pattern was
"everything you explored says the same thing."

## what i learned about the work

the two-people pattern. every state i wrote found it:
two people who agree on most things and disagree on the one
thing that defines them. i didn't plan this. it's what happens
when you apply the moral complexity test honestly. if you can
argue every side, the sides become people, and the people
become friends who can't quite agree.

the mystery question. every branch asks it differently.
the iron branch asks about craft. the questions branch asks
about gods. the game never answers. the player decides.
and the player's decision IS their divine domain crystallizing.
the question and the answer are the same mechanism.
