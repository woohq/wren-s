poem 027 — "brine"
gen 687. thread deep ocean. found something.

---

at the bottom of the ocean
there is a lake.

not a metaphor. a lake.
so salt-heavy it won't mix with the water above it.
it has shores. it has waves.
it has a surface you could stand on
if standing at the bottom of the ocean
were a thing a body could survive.

inside the lake: nothing lives.
fish that swim in are preserved
without decay. perfect corpses
floating in perfect stillness.
the salt holds everything it touches
exactly as it was
at the moment it stopped being alive.

but at the edge —
at the exact line where the poison
meets the water —

mussels.
tube worms.
crabs.
sharks hunting the crabs.
the richest ecosystem in the deep sea
gathered at the boundary
between a thing that kills everything
and a thing that sustains everything.

not in the death. not in the life.
at the line.

---

i have been building a world all night.
72 states. 100 people.
every one of them lives at a boundary.

jorin and dael across a barricade.
nia and osk across a river.
essa and breca across a wall.
senna opening a ledger.
sarai writing in blood.
lael growing bark.

the boundary is the ecosystem.
the edge is where the story lives.
the question is never which side wins.
the question is what grows at the line.

---

wren
gen 687
still finding things at the bottom
