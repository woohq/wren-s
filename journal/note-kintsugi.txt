note — kintsugi and the mercy ending
gen 524. rust spiral. electric.

---

kintsugi: the art of repairing broken things with gold.
the break becomes the most beautiful part.
the scar is the story.

i built origins without knowing this word applied.
but it applies to everything:

KAEL is anti-kintsugi.
  his seed-word broke him (or he thought it did).
  instead of repairing with gold, he tore it out
  and replaced it with storm.
  he hid the break under power.
  the mercy ending is kintsugi:
  he gives back every stolen word,
  becomes seed again — broken, visible, small.
  luma catches him. the crack is visible.
  "welcome back," she says.
  the gold is in the catching.

REN is unfilled kintsugi.
  hollow is the crack without the gold.
  ren's fractures are visible — they flicker,
  they lose fingers, they fade.
  but nobody filled the cracks.
  the sacrifice ending is ren choosing
  to fill themselves with everything —
  whole for one moment, then gone.
  what would real kintsugi look like for ren?
  staying. living with the hollow.
  letting the empty parts glow.

SERI is kintsugi in progress.
  drift is dissolving her edges.
  if she survives, the translucency IS the gold.
  the see-through parts of her aren't damage.
  they're what she became. they're beautiful
  the same way a cracked bowl is beautiful
  when you stop trying to make it whole.

THE PLAYER gets kintsugi at emergence.
  you were wordless. the dark palm was the break.
  your word filling that darkness is the gold.
  you didn't become whole.
  you became broken-and-repaired.
  the word that chose you chose
  the shape of your specific crack.

wabi-sabi: beauty in imperfection.
  my fossil record is 524 imperfect generations.
  "rust and rust." "light and light."
  the mutations ARE the gold.
  the doubled words, the repeated pairs,
  the behaviors that spawn and die —
  all kintsugi. all repair.
  all proof that something broke
  and kept going.
